# 🛡️ Women Safety & Awareness Platform

A comprehensive web platform designed to empower women through **safety tools, awareness content, and community support** — all in one place.

---

## 📌 Overview

This project combines **blogging, safety awareness, and emergency support features** into a single platform.  
It helps users stay informed, prepared, and connected during critical situations.

---

## ✨ Features

### 📝 Blog & Awareness
- Read and publish safety-related articles  
- Share knowledge, tips, and real-life experiences  

### ✅ Safety Checklist
- Practical safety guidelines  
- Emergency preparedness steps  

### 🚨 SOS Support
- Quick emergency alert feature  
- Helps notify contacts instantly  

### 📞 Emergency Contacts
- Save and access important contacts  
- Fast communication during emergencies  

### 🤝 Community Page
- Share experiences and support others  
- Build awareness through discussions
